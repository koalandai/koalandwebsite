The Problem

Your hotel generates decisions
every hour. Most are made blind.

Hotels leave 8 to 15% of potential revenue unrealised through slow, siloed decision-making. By the time a signal is noticed, analysed, discussed, and approved, your competitor has already moved. koaland.ai closes that gap.

01
Decision paralysis

Revenue managers switch between 12 tabs, three dashboards, and two spreadsheets to make a single pricing call. Most signals are missed entirely. The ones that aren't take days to act on, long after the window has closed.

02
Commission leakage

OTAs take 18 to 22% of every booking while your direct channel sits underleveraged. Without active, data-backed distribution strategy, you are subsidising your competitors' growth every single day.

03
Institutional amnesia

When your Revenue Manager leaves, years of pricing intuition leave with them. Seasonal playbooks, market patterns, channel preferences. Gone on their last day. Every new hire starts from zero.
